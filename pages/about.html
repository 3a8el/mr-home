The Mr. Home Story

Crafting Wood Legacies, One Project at a Time.

Welcome to Mr. Home, where our passion for wood craftsmanship transforms your space into a true sanctuary. We are dedicated manufacturers who believe that the quality of your finished wood pieces defines your home's elegance and comfort.

Mr. Home's Journey

Our journey began in 2020 when our founders—a designer and a specialized manufacturer—united to form Mr. Kitchen, dedicated to high-quality wood finishing and transforming kitchens with both beauty and function.

This foundation of design and manufacturing precision strengthened, leading to the establishment of our own factory. By 2023, we expanded our offerings, evolving into Mr. Home, a comprehensive hub for all custom wood finishing needs.
